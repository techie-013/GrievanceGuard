Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GrievanceGuard.

How do I register a complaint with GrievanceGuard?
You can register a complaint through multiple channels: (1) Use our web portal or mobile app, (2) Speak to our AI assistant via voice call, (3) Send an SMS to our dedicated number, or (4) Visit a designated citizen service center. The AI will guide you through providing necessary details like issue type, location, and description.
How long does it take to resolve a grievance?
Resolution time varies based on the complexity of the issue and department responsiveness. Simple issues like garbage collection are typically resolved within 24-48 hours, while complex infrastructure problems may take longer. You will receive an estimated resolution timeline when you file your complaint, and you can track progress in real-time.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, GrievanceGuard employs enterprise-grade security measures including data encryption, secure servers, and compliance with data protection regulations. Your personal information is only used for grievance resolution and is not shared with third parties without your consent, except with the concerned government department necessary for resolving your issue.
What if my complaint is not resolved satisfactorily?
If you're not satisfied with the resolution, you can escalate the complaint through the platform. The system will automatically route it to higher authorities in the department. You can also provide feedback that will be used for performance evaluation and continuous improvement of services.
Can I use GrievanceGuard in regional languages?
Absolutely! GrievanceGuard supports 12 major Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Odia, Punjabi, Assamese, and Urdu. You can interact with our AI assistant in your preferred language through both text and voice commands.
